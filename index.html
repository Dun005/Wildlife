African Elephants

The African elephant is the World largest land mammal. Weighing utpto 6000kg(6.6tonnes)and measuring upto 3.3m(10ft) A combination of the upper lip and nose,an African elephants truck is extremely versatile

Elephants uses their trunk for:

  • detecting vibration

  • breathing

  • caressing their young

  • sucking up water

  • grasping objects.
The tips of their trunk is comprised of two opposable extensions, or fingers which allows for extremedexterity.

Elephants are grouped in so many categories that include :

  1. The Indian elephant

  2. Asian Elephant

  3. African Bush elephant

  4. Sri-Lankan Elephant

  5. Straight-Tusked Elephants

African Lions

The lion (panthera leo) is a species in the family Felidae. It is vastly known and defined as the king of the jungle or the african pride. It is:
muscular
Deep chested
Cat with a short round head

The lion is sexually dimorphic;where the males are larger than female with a typical weight ranges of 150 to 250 kg.Male lions have a prominent mane,which is the most recognisable features of the species.A lion pride consists of afew adult males, related females and cubs.Groups of female lions typically hunt together,preying mostly on larger ungulates. The species is an apex and keystone predator,although they scavenge when opportunity occurs.
